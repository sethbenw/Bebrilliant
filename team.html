Jake Freedman

Founder and CEO of Dovr Media.

Jake Freedman

Founder and CEO of Dovr Media.

Jake founded Dovr and its family of companies with his main focus is making sure the talented staff is on the right track and taking responsibility for tasks they have on a day-to-day basis. Before Dovr Media, Jake went to Northeastern University where he graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Finance and Financial Management Services and Supply Chain. After finishing up his degree Jake worked for Draper, a laboratory that specializes in the design, development, and deployment of advanced technology solutions to problems in national security, space exploration, health care and energy. While at Draper Jake picked up an interest in immersive technologies and saw how new applications of technologies benefits businesses. This interest turned into a passion and Jake left Draper to begin lecturing about immersive technology at MIT. From here he further buried himself in immersive technology by creating Dovr Media. At first Dovr was just creating virtual showrooms for businesses, but now has evolved to enable brands with our Brilliant® marketing platform. Outside of work Jake loves to play golf and hang out with his dog, Ziggy.

Rich Kehoe

Chief Sales Officer at Dovr Media.

As our Chief Sales Officer, Rich is in charge of our customer relations, finding new clients, and building the brand name in world markets. Before he came to Dovr Media, Rich earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology at Rowan University. During Rich’s time at Rowan he was the President of Sigma Alpha Epsilon, a member of the Executive Board for the Inter Greek Committee, and the founder of the Rowan University Dance Marathon. Rich has leadership experience in Sales & Marketing on both the professional and philanthropic level at Pharmacy Times and Insight Global. Outside of work Rich loves to play soccer and visit the Jersey Shore.
